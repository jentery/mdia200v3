Images

Material agents for transforming
light or graphics into pictures or files

Recall the content industry: images help us
circulate visuals, including otherwise
fleeting visual impressions

Elements of Images

line, value, shape, form, space, colour, texture

Principles of Images

balance, contrast, movement, emphasis,
pattern, proportion, unity

Comics

A format for nesting images
in a single space (page or screen)

Usually image + text
(except wordless or "silent" comics)

Elements of Comics

bleed, caption, frame, grid, gutter, horizon line,
linear perspective, panel, panel density,
sound effects, speech bubbles, splash, spread,text box, thought balloon, verbal track, visual track
